Boiled Potatoes with Chives

How to Make Boiled Potatoes with Chives

These delicious boiled potatoes with butter and chives are simple to make. I like to use small red potatoes or new potatoes.

Ingredients for Boiled Potatoes with Chives

  • 2 pounds very small red potatoes, scrubbed
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into several pieces and softened
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh chives
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions for cooking Boiled Potatoes with Chives

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, 10 to 15 minutes. Drain and let sit until cool enough to handle, 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Cut potatoes in half and place into a serving bowl.
  4. Add butter, chives, salt, and pepper; toss until butter melts and potatoes are well coated.
